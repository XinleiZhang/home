## Biography

I am an Associate Researcher at the State Key Laboratory of Nonlinear Mechanics, Institute of Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (since 2024). My research focuses on **AI-enpowered turbulence modeling and shape optimization**.

I received my B.S. (2013) and M.S. (2016) in Mechanical Engineering from Zhengzhou University, and my Ph.D. (2020) from Arts et Métiers ParisTech (ENSAM), France. During my doctoral studies, I was a visiting researcher at the Department of Aerospace and Ocean Engineering, Virginia Tech, USA. After my PhD, I conducted postdoctoral research at the Institute of Mechanics, CAS (2020–2024).

## Research Interests

- Data-driven turbulence modeling
- Ensemble Kalman methods and data assimilation
- Machine learning for computational fluid dynamics
- High-fidelity shape optimization
