## Biography

I am an Associate Researcher at the State Key Laboratory of Nonlinear Mechanics, Institute of Mechanics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (since 2024). My research focuses on **data-driven turbulence modeling**.

I received my B.S. (2013) and M.S. (2016) in Mechanical Engineering from Zhengzhou University, and my Ph.D. (2020) from Arts et Métiers ParisTech (ENSAM), France, supported by the China Scholarship Council. During my doctoral studies, I was a visiting researcher at the Department of Aerospace and Ocean Engineering, Virginia Tech, USA. After my PhD, I conducted postdoctoral research at the Institute of Mechanics, CAS (2020–2024), under the supervision of Academician Guowei He.

I have published 39 SCI papers, including 24 as first/corresponding author, in leading journals such as *Journal of Fluid Mechanics*, *Journal of Computational Physics*, and *AIAA Journal*. Two papers were selected as Featured Articles by *Physics of Fluids* and *Communications in Computational Physics*, respectively. My work has been cited 708 times (568 non-self citations), with an H-index of 16. In 2022, I was selected for the 8th Young Talent Support Program of the China Association for Science and Technology (CAST).

## Research Interests

- Data-driven turbulence modeling
- Ensemble Kalman methods and data assimilation
- Machine learning for computational fluid dynamics
- Large-eddy simulation and wall modeling
- Cavitating flow simulation
